## Format Notes

1. **Checkboxes**: Use `[ ]` for tracking status
2. **Categories**: Group by type (must-clear, documents, payoffs, etc.)
3. **Contact Info**: Include where to obtain each item
4. **Status Tracking**: Multiple stages for complex items
5. **Remaining Exceptions**: Clearly separate items that stay on policy
