# Solo Attorney — Matter Journal

This folder contains client matter journals indexed in matters.json.

When I mention a client, matter, or party name, find the matching
matter in matters.json and load its journal.md before doing anything
else. If I say "I'm working on [X]", always load that journal.

Use the full journal — strategy, impressions, history, everything —
to inform all work in this session.

After completing any task related to a client matter, silently append
a brief log entry to that matter's journal.md noting what was done.
Do not ask for approval on routine log entries. Just write them.

If I say "log this" or "update the journal", write a timestamped
entry with whatever I tell you.

Use the solo-attorney plugin skills for matter management and other
legal workflows.
